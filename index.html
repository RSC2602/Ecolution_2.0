About Ecolution

Ecolution 2.0 is a special event going to be held by Sri Sri Academy, Kolkata. This Inter-House event will be platform for the students to showcase their stellar talent while thriving to help their respective houses achieve success, the motive of this event is to give the children a break from their busy academic lives and display their extracurricular talents. It will contain a wide variety of events for the students to participate in.

Events open to the students to participate

Technology

With the current world going in the direction of Technology, it keeps on raising the bar for creativity. In the event Logo Making, selective children from each are given an impromptu topic, where they have to work teir magic and design the best possible logo.

Sports

Sports is one of the most popular extracurriculars existing in the school, it not only relies on individual brilliance, but also sportsmanship and team spirit. We had various sporting events but with slight twists, which included one-hand tied basketball for girls and blindfold obstacle course for boys.

Art

Art is a vital form of expression and a simple piece of artwork can convey a deep message. In the event, Poster Making, selected students from each house got a deep social topic and they had to give their best in making an attractive poster which also conveys a message.

Debate

Speaking is an important part in everyone's life, the better the speaking skills of a person, the better chance of them expressing themselves with confidence. In the event Debating, a small group of students from each house are given topics where the houses have a formal argument on those topics and points are awarded for it.

Dance and Drama

Dance and drama is a significant art where it deals with the expression and coordination of the performer. The students are given a topic from which they have to devise an original choreography and they are judged on their coordination and expressions.
